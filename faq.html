Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I get started as a client?

Simply sign up for a free account, create your profile, and start posting jobs or browsing services. You can post your first job in minutes!

How do I become a freelancer?

Sign up and select "Freelancer" during registration. Complete your profile, add your skills and portfolio, and start applying to jobs or creating service packages.

How are payments processed?

We use secure escrow services. When you place an order, funds are held safely until work is completed and approved. Once you approve the work, payment is released to the freelancer.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

You can request revisions from the freelancer. If issues persist, our support team can help mediate and ensure a fair resolution. Your payment is protected until you're satisfied.

Are there any fees?

Clients pay a small service fee on completed projects (5% for free accounts, 3% for Professional, 1% for Enterprise). Freelancers pay a commission based on their earnings tier.

How do I contact a freelancer?

Once you place an order or hire a freelancer, you can communicate through our built-in messaging system. This keeps all project communication in one place.

Can I cancel an order?

Yes, you can cancel an order if work hasn't started. If work has begun, you may need to pay for completed milestones. Contact support for assistance with cancellations.

How do ratings and reviews work?

After completing a project, both clients and freelancers can leave ratings and reviews. This helps build trust and helps others make informed decisions.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we follow industry best practices to protect your personal and payment information.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Please contact our support team.
